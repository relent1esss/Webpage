About Us

   Unicure Remedies Pvt. ltd. is a pharmaceutical manufacturing company based out of Vadodara in the state of Gujarat. It banks over four decades of experience in a growing industry. Powered by a dynamic team, our marketing-oriented structure is aimed at providing integrated solutions in production as well as promotion. With a growing presence in both domestic and international markets, we cater to various therapeutic segments, like ORT, Antiprotozoal, GI Tract, Cardiovascular, Urology, Anti Allergens, Contraception and HRT. We have been successfully exporting General tablets, Hormonal tablets and Oral powders since the past three decades. We have a loyal customer base that benefits from the quality and cost advantages we offer, along with end-to-end service support.
